DeepTempo Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. The purpose of this policy is to let you know how we handle the information collected through your use of the website www.deeptempo.io (the "Site").

What's Covered Under this Privacy Policy:

  • This policy applies to the Site and all of the services offered by DeepTempo, LLC ("DeepTempo", the "Company" "We", "Us", "Ours")
  • This policy covers how We treat Personal Information that We collect and receive. Personal information is any information about you that is personally identifiable (like your name, address, email address, or phone number) and that is not commonly, publicly available.
  • This policy does not apply to practices of companies that are not owned or controlled by Us, or to people who are not employed or managed by Us.

Information: Collection & Usage

We collect personal information when you register with the Site.

  • When you register, we may ask for Personal Information such as your name, email, address, and date of birth. When you register with DeepTempo and sign in to the Site, you are not anonymous to us.
  • We may collect information about your transactions with us and with some of our customers and advertisers.

DeepTempo may use the Personal Information for the purposes described in section 3 below, as well as:

  • Customizing the advertising and content you see on the Site;
  • Auditing, research, and analysis to maintain, protect, and improve the Site and the Services;
  • Ensuring the technical functioning of the Site and our network;
  • Fulfilling your requests for products and services, including the development of new services;
  • Contacting you;
  • Conducting research; and
  • Provide anonymous reporting for customer, providers, or clients.

We automatically receive and record information on our Site server logs, including your IP address, the web request, and one or more cookies which may uniquely identify your browser. These server logs may also include your browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request.

Cookies:

A cookie is a small file containing a string of characters that uniquely identifies your browser.

  • When you visit the Site, we may set and access cookies on your computer;
  • If your browser is set to refuse all cookies, some features of the Site may not function properly;
  • We use cookies to improve the quality of our Site and services we offer by storing user preferences and tracking user trends;
  • When you send email or other communication to us, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests, and improve the Site and our services.

We may present links in a format that enables us to track when the links are followed. We may use this information to improve the quality of our content and advertising.

Disclosure and Use of Personal Information

DeepTempo processes Personal Information only for the purposes for which it was collected and in accordance with this policy or any other applicable service-specific privacy notice. We retain the right to review our data collection, storage, and processing practices to ensure that we only collect, store, and process the Personal Information needed to provide or improve the Site or our services. We take reasonable steps to ensure that the Personal Information we process is accurate, complete, and current, but it is incumbent upon our users so update and/or correct their Personal Information whenever necessary.

Disclosing Personal Information

We do not rent, sell, or share Personal Information about you with other people or companies except when we have your written permission.

Notwithstanding the above, if we have a good faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of such information is reasonably necessary, we may share your Personal Information under the following circumstances:

  • To satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request (which may include, but is not limited to, responding to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or establishing or exercising our legal rights or defending against legal claims);
  • To enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations thereof;
  • To detect, prevent, investigate, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues; or
  • To protect against imminent harm to the rights, property or safety of DeepTempo, the Site, its users, or the public, as required or permitted by law, and to protect against potential threats to the physical safety of another person.

We may share certain pieces of aggregated, non-Personal Information with third parties (such as number of users who click on a particular advertisement), but that information shall not identify you individually.

If DeepTempo becomes involved in a merger, acquisition, or any form of sale of any or all of its assets, we will provide notice to you before your Personal Information is transferred and may become subject to a different privacy policy.

Using Personal Information:

We do not provide any Personal Information to an advertiser when you interact with or view a targeted ad, however, by interacting with or viewing the ad, you are consenting to the possibility that the advertiser will make the assumption that you meet the targeting criteria used to display the ad.

Information Security, Confidentiality

We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to and/or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of date. These include (but may not be limited to): a) regular internal reviews of our data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures; and b) Physical security measures to guard against unauthorized access to systems where we store personal data.

We have physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards that comply with federal regulations to protect Personal Information about you.
