Our Mission

Skidaway is reimagining the cyber security industry. We remain in stealth and are working with large design partners currently.

Our Values

At Skidaway, we strive to be extremely transparent and fact-based in our approaches. We are also not afraid to think differently or to poke fun at ourselves our crazy ideas. We are also deeply aware of how crucial it is that, together, we start to turn back the tide of increasingly sophisticated cyber attacks.

About Us

Skidaway was founded by a team with diverse experiences including having built security operations teams and broadly deployed enterprise data infrastructure solutions. We came together in 2023 to reimagine cyber security from first principles, committed to changing the game to give more insight and freedom of choice to enterprises while building solutions that adversaries will find are always one step ahead of them.

The Problem

Cyber security spending now exceeds $200bn per year. And the costs of cyber attacks now exceed $6trn per year. Meanwhile the cyber security industry, in general, takes a traditional approach to business, selling customers top down into lock-in based products and business models.
